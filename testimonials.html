Our Mission

We are dedicated to giving people a space and the power to unwind and reset from the stress of everyday life by allowing them to engage with the power of the frisbee. Whether work is chaotic, you are a new parent navigating the world of child-rearing, or just want to take a break from it all, Throw To Grow, offers everyone the chance to toss all the stress away. Throw To Grow members haev a wide range of experience ranging from expert tossers to brand-new beginners. All you need is that courage to make that first toss and enjoy the company of those who also took the first step in putting their mental health first. We are so happy you are interested in joining us. Each throw creates a smile. Let us create one for you.
