Our Mission

We are dedicated to giving people a space and the power to unwind and reset from the stress of everyday life by allowing them to engage with the power of the frisbee. Whether work is chaotic, a new parent navigating the world of child-rearing, or just want to take a break from it, Throw To Grow, offers everyone the chance to toss it all away. Whether you are an expert tosser or have never thrown a frisbee in your life, there is always room for friendship at Throw to Grow. Bring a friend, bring an entire office, or just yourself! All you need is that courage to make that first toss and enjoy the company of those who also took the first in putting their mental health first. We are so happy you are interested in joining us. Each throw creates a smile. Let us create one for you.
